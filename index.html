Did you know? In 2020, buildings accounted for 39% of global CO2 emissions, making homes a major contributor to pollution.

For decades, traditional homes have consumed excessive energy, allowed outdoor pollution to seep indoors, and struggled to maintain a comfortable temperature naturally.

The consequences? Rising electricity bills, poor indoor air quality, and a growing environmental impact.

With HOMIE, small changes at your home create a big impact on our planet.
